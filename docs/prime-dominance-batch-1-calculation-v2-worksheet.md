# Main model consequence

V2 fixes the Khabib problem without making the formula vibes-based.

The new shape makes sense:

- Khabib and Jon are the top Prime Dominance cases in this batch.
- DJ still gets massive credit for 11 defenses but is capped by flyweight strength.
- Amanda is just behind DJ because her peak finishing and 7 cross-belt defenses are huge, but Pena caps her.
- GSP gets strong WW/round-control credit but Serra keeps him below DJ/Amanda.
- Anderson rises a lot but is capped by Weidman + MW strength.
- Volk and Max rise because current scores overpunish later losses and undercredit modern FW difficulty.
